I am thrilled to share with you the results of my latest data analytics project, where I analyzed a Data Professional Survey. As a data professional, I found it fascinating to explore the current state of the industry and uncover insights that could help shape the future of our field.

My analysis focused on several key areas, including job roles, salaries, skills, and career satisfaction. Here are some of the most significant findings from my research:

According to the 630 data professionals that took the survey,

Data science is the most popular job role among data professionals, followed by data engineer and data architect.

The average salary for data professionals ranges from $20,000 to about $200,000, depending on their job role and level of experience.
